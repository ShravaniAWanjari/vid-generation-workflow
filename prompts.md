# Suggested Video Generation Prompts

These prompts are heavily optimized for the **Wan2.1-I2V-14B** model to ensure the product container stays completely static while the specified ingredients organically bloom and grow around it.

You can copy and paste the prompt corresponding to your product directly into the Streamlit UI.

---

Video is for reference. generate a similar one using Image and these ingredient elements

### 1. Hydrating Gel Cream

**Ingredients:** Tamarind fruit, Pink Rose flower (Front) | Tamarind leaf, Rose leaf (Back)

```text
A cinematic macro commercial shot. The camera is positioned in a static placement, framing the product container in the center. In a seamless, slow pan/zoom out motion, the product container remains perfectly still, static, and unaltered in the center. As the camera slowly moves, distinct, highly realistic raw Tamarind fruit and Pink Rose flowers organically bloom and unfurl near the front bottom of the static product container. Simultaneously, lush Tamarind leaves and Rose leaves grow and emerge at the back, slightly above the ground. The ingredients must be clearly separated and identifiable, not mashed together. The product container itself must stay completely static, with its label and shape perfectly still and unchanged throughout the entire video. Highly detailed textures, soft studio lighting, high-end commercial advertisement style.
```
